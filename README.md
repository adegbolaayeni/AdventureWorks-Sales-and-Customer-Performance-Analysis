# AdventureWorks; Sales and Customer Performance Analysis

A multi-page Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance, 
customer behavior, and territory revenue across the AdventureWorks 
dataset (2011–2014).

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

| Page | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Executive Overview | Top-level KPIs and revenue split |
| Sales Trends | Monthly revenue, YoY growth |
| Territory & Region | Revenue by territory and country |
| Customer Insights | Segmentation and retention analysis |
| Salesperson Performance | Quota vs. actual, commissions |

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

- **Total Revenue:** $123M across 31.5K orders
- **Customers:** 19.1K | Avg Order Value: $3.9K
- **Top Territory:** Southwest ($27.2M)
- **Dominant Region:** North America (72.4% revenue share)
- **YoY Growth:** +166% (2011→2012), +30% (2012→2013)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Online vs Offline:** Offline orders drive 73.7% of revenue 
  but only 12% of volume — indicating high-value B2B offline 
  transactions
- **Customer Retention:** Only 1 in 3 customers return, 
  representing a significant growth opportunity
- **B2B vs B2C:** Business customers are just 7% of the base 
  but generate 74% of revenue
- **Salesperson Performance:** Every quota-assigned salesperson 
  exceeded their target — some by over 1,000%
- **Revenue Decline in 2014:** Reflects Jan–Jun data only, 
  not a true annual decline
